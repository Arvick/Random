  1. What is COVID-19?

    2. The Basics

    COVID-19 stands for:

    1. COrona
    2. VIrus
    3. Disease
    4. 19- 2019(aka the year it originated)

    What is it?

    1. A strain OF a type of virus
    2. Coronavirus- The Family
      • Viruses that cause illnesses from a small cold to SARS
    3. Some people call it “Coronavirus” due to its central role in current society
    4. "Novel" = New
      • That just means it’s a new strain of the virus we’re not familiar with (has different effects)
